SET UP FOR VOR APPROACH

PRESS “B” AND “D” TO CALIBRATE ALT AND NAV

SET NAV1 TO 116.8

SET OBS TO 338 DEGREES

SET ADF FREQ TO 281

SET ADF HEADING TO 338 DEGREES

TURN ON MARKER, TRIM ELEVATOR AND RUDDER

 

OPTIONAL GPS FPL TO KSEA

PRESS “DIRECT TO WAYPOINT” BUTTON (--D-- )

ENTER KSEA WITH KNOBS

PRESS “ENT” ON GPS

SELECT “ACTIVATE”

PRESS “PROC”

SELECT “SELECT APPROACH”

SCROLL DOWN TO “VOR34R” AND “ENT”

SELECT “FACTS” FOR THE VOR AND “ENT”

SELECT “ACTIVATE”

 

PROCEED TO FACTS

ALTITUDE >5000(5050) FT., SPEED 100 KIAS

HEADING 66 DEGREES

INTERCEPT 338 DEGREE RADIAL

AT 16.9 NM (FACTS), START DESCENT TO 3000 FT.

AT 10.9 NM (MILLT), START DESCENT TO 1600 FT.

AT 4.2 NM (ODD), START DESCENT TO 890 FT.

WHEN TOLD, FLY MISSED APPROACH PROC.

CLIMB TO >2100 (2150) FT.

MAINTAIN 339 DEGREE HEADING UNTIL “PARKK” AT 5.9 NM

 

PROCEED TO SEA VOR

CHANGE OBS TO 140 DEGREES

TURN LEFT, HEADING TO 160 DEGREES

CLIMB TO 3000 FT.

INTERCEPT 140 DEGREE RADIAL

 

CROSS SEA VOR, THEN

CHANGE OBS TO 320 DEGREES

TURN RIGHT, HEADING TO 170 DEGREES

START 1 MINUTE TIMER, TIME TO 1:05

TURN LEFT AND INTERCEPT 320 DEGREE RADIAL

 

CROSS SEA VOR AGAIN, THEN

TURN RIGHT, HEADING 140 DEGREES

START 1 MINUTE TIMER, TIME TO 1:05

TURN RIGHT AND INTERCEPT 320 DEGREE RADIAL

 

CROSS SEA VOR AGAIN AND HEAD TO NOLLA

 SET NAV1 TO 110.9

 SET OBS TO 130 DEGREES

 SET ADF FREQUENCY TO 362, ADF HEADING TO 326

 TURN TO HEADING 326 DEGREES

 DESCEND TO 2200 FT. (2250)

 

 OPTIONAL GPS FPL TO KBFI

 PRESS “DIRECT TO WAYPOINT” BUTTON (--D--)

 ENTER KBFI WITH KNOBS

 PRESS “ENT” ON GPS

 SELECT “ACTIVATE”

 PRESS “PROC”

 SELECT “SELECT APPROACH”

 SCROLL DOWN TO “ILS13R” AND “ENT”

 SELECT “NOLLA” FOR THE VOR AND “ENT”

 SELECT “ACTIVATE”

 

 CROSS NOLLA OUTER MARKER AT 8.1 NM

 AT 8.1 NM TURN TO HEADING 310 DEGREES

 FLY FOR 1 MIN. OUTBOUND LOCALIZER (~10.1 NM)

 TURN LEFT TO 265 DEGREES

 FLY  STRAIGHT FOR 1:10 MIN.

 TURN RIGHT TO 085 DEGREES

 INTERCEPT INBOUND LOCALIZER AT 130 DEGREES

 

 STAY ON LOCALIZER

 FLAPS 10 DEGREES < 110 KIAS

 FLAPS 15 – 30 DEGREES < 85 KIAS

TRIM ELEVATOR AND RUDDER

INTERCEPT GLIDESLOPE

 ADJUST THROTTLE/PITCH FOR ~-484 FPM, SPEED 60 KIAS

 STAY ON GLIDESLOPE

 RAISE SEAT 2 CLICKS (SHIFT ENTER)

 

 LAND

 AFTER CROSSING RUNWAY, FLARE TO 14 DEGREES

 MAINTAIN POWER ~ 55 KIAS

 STALL SPEED AT FULL FLAPS IS 40 KIAS!

 TOUCH DOWN AT LESS THAN -50 FPM

 SLOWLY TOUCH NOSE WHEEL DOWN

 YOU HAVE 60 SEC. TO TOUCH DOWN

 YOU HAVE 30 SEC. TO STOP
