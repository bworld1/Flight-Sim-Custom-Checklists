ATC / ATIS CHECK IN AND FMC CONFIGURATION

 

 

 

ON SIMICON PANEL, CLICK “MCP”, RESIZE AND LOCATE

 

 

 

ON SIMICON PANEL, CLICK “ATC”, RESIZE AND LOCATE

 

 

 

ON ATC MENU, SELECT “TUNE ATC CLEARANCE”

 

 

 

ON ATC, REQUEST IFR CLEARANCE AND ACKNOWLEDGE

 

 

 

ON MCP/AP, SET USING THUMBWHEEL, ADJUST ALTITUDE KNOB TO ASSIGNED ALTITUDE (LEFT CLICK IN DISPLAY WILL MOVE NUMBERS FASTER) (ALSO, SELECT “THOUSANDS” WITH OUTER KNOB FOR FASTER ALTITUDE CHANGES)

 

 

 

ON ATC, SELECT “TUNE ATIS”

 

 

 

RECORD ‘RUNWAYS IN USE’ FOR CONFIGURING CDU LATER

 

 

 

RECORD “ALTIMETER” AND “TEMPERATURE”

 

 

 

ON ATC, SELECT “TUNE GROUND” TO TURN OFF ATIS SCROLLING

 

 

 

ON SIMICON PANEL, CLICK “EFIS”

 

 

 

ON EFIS, SET ALTIMETER TO ATIS ALTIMETER VALUE THAT YOU RECORDED USING KNOB BELOW “BARO”, MONITOR CHANGES IN PFD DISPLAY

 

 

 

MOVE COCKPIT VIEW TO DISPLAY MCP / AP, OR ON SIMICON PANEL, CLICK “MCP”. LOOK UP MAGNETIC HEADING FOR THE ACTIVE RUNWAYS YOU RECORDED AND SET MCP / AP HEADING TO THAT VALUE

 

 

 

ON SIMICON PANEL, CLICK “CDU”, RESIZE AND LOCATE

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK “MENU”

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK R-LSK (RIGHT LINE SELECT KEY) FOR “EFIS CTL” ON

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK R-LSK FOR “EFIS DSP CTL” ON

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK R-LSK FOR “SETUP”

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK L-LSK (LEFT LINE SELECT KEY) FOR “PAUSE AT TOP OF DESCENT” OFF

 

 

 

RECORD CURRENT COG (CENTER OF GRAVITY)

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK L-LSK FOR “MENU”

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK L-LSK FOR “FMC”

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK “INIT REF”, THEN L-LSK FOR “INDEX”, IF INIT / REF INDEX PAGE IS NOT ALREADY DISPLAYED

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK L-LSK “IDENT” CHECK, VERIFY

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK R-LSK “POS INIT”

 

 

 

CLICK “CLR” MULTIPLE TIMES UNTIL THERE IS NO TEXT ALONG THE BOTTOM OF THE CDU DISPLAY

 

 

 

ON CDU, TO SET INITIAL POSITION, CLICK R-LSK “GPS POS” DATA TO COPY DATA TO BOTTOM OF CDU DISPLAY

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK R-LSK “INIT POS” TO PASTE GPS DATA INTO INIT POS EMPTY SQUARES, IF COMPLETE, THE LINE WILL DISAPPEAR. IF THERE ARE NO EMPTY SQUARES, THEN THE “ADIRU” HAS NOT BEEN INITALIZED ON THE OVERHEAD (IT IS OFF)

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK R-LSK “ROUTE” OR CLICK “RTE” BUTTON

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK L-LSK “REQUEST <SEND” TO LOAD A FLIGHT PLAN

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK L-LSK TO LOAD A CAPTAIN SIM FLIGHT PLAN IF ONE IS DISPLAYED, OR CLICK R-LSK “FLTSIM FPLS” TO LOAD AN FSX FLTPLAN

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK “NEXT OR PREV PAGE” TO SCROLL THRU FLTPLANS

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK L-LSK “ TO SELECT A FLTPLAN

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK R-LSK “ACTIVATE” TO ACTIVATE FLTPLAN

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK “EXEC” TO EXECUTE

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK ON “DEP ARR” TO SELECT DESTINATION RUNWAY

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK “PREV / NEXT PAGE” TO SCROLL THRU PAGES TO SEE ALL OF THE AVAILABLE RUNWAYS

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK R-LSK TO SELECT ONE OF THE ACTIVE RUNWAYS THAT WAS RECORDED EARLIER FROM THE ATIS

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK “EXEC” TO EXECUTE

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK L-LSK “INDEX” TO VIEW DEP / ARR INDEX

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK R-LSK “ARR” FOR THE DESTINATION AIRPORT (BE SURE IT IS THE DESTINATION AIRPORT)

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK “PREV / NEXT PAGE” TO SCROLL THRU PAGES TO SEE ALL AVAILABLE RUNWAYS

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK R-LSK TO SELECT AN ILS RUNWAY THAT YOU WANT TO LAND ON

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK “EXEC” TO EXECUTE

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK “INIT REF” TO CONTINUE CONFIGURATION

 

 

 

CDU DISPLAY SHOULD BE ON “PERF INIT” AT THIS POINT, ELSE CLICK ON L-LSK “INDEX”, THEN L-LSK “PERF”

 

 

 

BOTTOM OF CDU DISPLAY SHOULD BE BLANK, ELSE CLICK ON “CLR” TO CLEAR ALL TEXT AT BOTTOM

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK ON NUMBERS PAD TO ENTER THE GROSS WEIGHT IN LBS X 1000 WITH ONE DECIMAL POINT (300,000 WOULD BE 300.0) USE “CLR” TO BACKSPACE IF NEEDED

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK L-LSK “GROSS WT” TO PASTE NUMBER, IT ALSO CALCULATES THE ZFW AUTOMATICALLY

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK ON NUMBERS PAD TO ENTER THE FUEL RESERVES YOU PLANNED IN THOUSANDS WITH ONE DECIMAL POINT (10,000 WOULD BE 10.0) USE “CLR” TO BACKSPACE

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK L-LSK “RESERVES” TO PASTE NUMBER

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK ON NUMBERS PAD TO ENTER THE CRUISING ALTITUDE FROM THE FLTPLAN IN HUNDREDS WITH NO DECIMAL POINT (33,000 WOULD BE 330) USE “CLR” TO BACKSPACE

 

 

 

ON CDU CLICK R-LSK “CRZ ALT” TO PASTE NUMBER

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK ON NUMBERS PAD TO ENTER THE COST INDEX FROM 1 TO 999. THIS IS A COMPANY NUMBER REPRESENTING HOW ECONOMICAL THE PLANE WILL FLY, TYPICAL VALUE IS 100. USE “CLR” TO BACKSPACE

 

 

 

ON CDU CLICK R-LSK “COST INDEX” TO PASTE NUMBER

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK R-LSK “THRUST LIM”

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK ON NUMBERS PAD TO ENTER THE VALUE THAT IS DISPLAYED ON THE CDU FOR OAT (OUTSIDE TEMPERATURE), USE “CLR” TO BACKSPACE

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK L-LSK “SEL” TO PASTE TMEPERATURE VALUE

 

 

 

ON CDU, CHECK THE R-LSK “TO N1” (TAKE OFF N1 PERCENTAGE, REPRESENTS HOW HIGH THE COMPUTER WILL SET THE THROTTLE AUTOMATICALLY ON TAKE OFF, IT SHOULD NOT BE ABOVE 100%)

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK L-LSK NEXT TO “TO”, “-10%”, OR -20% WHICH DISPLAYS THE HIGHEST N1 VALUE THAT IS BELOW 100%

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK R-LSK FOR “CLB”, “CLB 1”, OR “CLB2”

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK R-LSK “TAKEOFF”

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK ON NUMBERS PAD TO ENTER 15 (DEGREES OF FLAPS). USE “CLR” TO BACKSPACE

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK L-LSK “FLAPS” TO PASTE NUMBER

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK ON NUMBERS PAD TO ENTER THE CENTER OF GRAVITY VALUE RECORDED EARLIER OR CHECK BY CLICKING “MENU” ON CDU, THEN R-LSK “SETUP”, THEN CHECKING VALUE OF “CURRENT COG”, THEN L-LSK “MENU, THEN L-LSK “FMC”, THEN L-LSK “TAKEOFF” TO GET BACK,  “CLR” TO BACKSPACE

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK L-LSK “CG” TO PASTE NUMBER

 

 

 

ON CDU, TO THE RIGHT OF CG IS NOW A “TRIM” VALUE FOR THE STABILIZER TRIM FOR TAKE OFF.

 

 

 

ON SIMICON PANEL, CLICK “DSP”, THEN ON DISPLAY PANEL, CLICK “LWR CNTR”

 

 

 

ON SIMICON PANEL, CLICK BLUE ICON FOR “LWR CNTR” DISPLAY TO ENLARGE IT

 

 

 

ON DISPLAY PANEL, CLICK “FCTL” TO DISPLAY FLIGHT CONTROLS, MONITOR THE “STAB” VALUE

 

 

 

IT IS EASIEST TO USE THE ELEVATOR TRIM ROCKER BUTTON ON YOUR YOKE WHILE VIEWING THE COCKPIT, OR ZOOM IN CLOSELY TO THE PEDESTAL TRIM LEVER AND TRIM INDICATOR. GRAB THE TRIM LEVER BY LEFT CLICKING, AND DRAG THE LEVER SLIGHTLY UP OR DOWN AND WATCH THE “STAB” VALUE IN THE “FCTL” DISPLAY. WHEN IT IS EQUAL TO THE  TRIM VALUE ON THE CDU, LET GO OF THE LEVER TO STOP THE VALUE FROM CHANGING. THE STABILIZER TRIM ANGLE IS NOW SET FOR TAKE OFF.

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK R-LSK “REF V1” TO ACTIVATE IT

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK R-LSK “REF VR” TO ACTIVATE IT

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK R-LSK “REF V2” TO ACTIVATE IT

 

 

 

ON MCP, USE THUMBWHEEL ON AIR SPEED DISPLAY AND SET TO V2 + 10 (THIS IS THE INITIAL SPEED AFTER LIFTING OFF)

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK ON L-LSK “INDEX”

 

 

 

ON CDU CLICK L-LSK “APPROACH”

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK R-LSK “ 25 DEGREES” FOR A 25 DEGREE LANDING AT VREF SPEED

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK R-LSK “FLAP / SPEED” TO PASTE NUMBERS. THE SPEED VALUE IS CALLED VREF, AND WE WILL LAND AT NO LESS THAN VREF + 5 KNOTS

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK “NAV RAD”

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK L-LSK “ ILS – MLS ONCE TO COPY THE DATA, THEN IMMEDIATELY CLICK THE SAME LSK AGAIN TO PASTE IT, THIS WILLSHOW LARGE NUMBERS NOW, MEANING THE ILS FREQUENCY IS NOW ACTIVE

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK “LEGS”, CLICKING “PREV / NEXT PAGE” WILL DISPLAY THE ENTIRE ROUTE

 

 

 

ON CDU, FIRST LINE SHOWS RUNWAY FOR TAKEOFF AND TO THE RIGHT SHOWS ALTITUDE AS -9998. SET ALTITUDE TO CURRENT ALTITUDE DISPLAYED ON PFD BY ENTERING DIGITS INTO BOTTOM OF CDU DISPLAY. USE LEADING ZEROES IF ALTITUDE IS LESS THAN 4 DIGITS (EX. 0030). THEN CLICK R-LSK OF RUNWAY LINE TO PASTE ALTITUDE VALUE

 

 

 

MOVE COCKPIT VIEW AND ZOOM IN SO THAT THE L INBD DISPLAY AND INBOARD DISPLAY PANEL BELOW IT ARE LARGE AND NEXT TO CDU DISPLAY

 

 

 

CHANGE INBD DISPLAY FROM “MFD” TO “NAV”

 

 

 

ON SIMICON, CLICK “EFIS”

 

 

 

ON “EFIS” PANEL, POINT AT MODE SELECTOR KNOB AND USE THUMBWHEEL TO CHANGE FROM “MAP” TO “PLN” (FLTPLAN) AND USE THE RANGE KNOB TO SEE PARTS OF ROUTE

 

 

 

ON CDU, IF ANY LINES SHOW “ROUTE DISCONTINUITY” THIS NEEDS TO BE CORRECTED. THE LINE WITH THE SQUARES IS EXPECTING CORRECT WAYPOINT DATA. ALSO, THERE MAY BE ERRONEOUS WAYPOINTS THAT NEED TO BE ERASED NEAR THE END OF THE ROUTE

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK L-LSK NEXT TO THE WAYPOINT THAT IS JUST BELOW THE LINE WITH THE EMPTY SQUARES TO COPY IT TO BOTTOM OF CDU

 

 

 

ON CDU, CLICK L-LSK NEXT TO THE LINE WITH THE EMPTY SQUARES TO PASTE THE WAYPOINT DATA.

 

 

 

ON THE CDU, CLICK “EXEC” TO EXECUTE, THE DISCONTINUITY MESSAGE SHOULD DISAPPEAR AND YOU SHOULD SEE CORRESPONDING CHANGES ON THE PLN VIEW OF THE L INBD DISPLAY

 

 

 

START ON THE LAST PAGE AND VIEW THE WAYPOINTS. IF ANY LINES HAVE ERRONEOUS WAYPOINTS, CLICK “DEL”, THEN CLICK THE L-LSK NEXT TO THE WAYPOINT TO DELETE, THEN CLICK “EXEC”.  THERE SHOULD BE NO WAYPOINTS AFTER THE DESTINATION RUNWAY! AT ANY TIME AND ESPECIALLY WHEN FINISHED, YOU CAN REVIEW ALL OF THE WAYPOINTS IN THE “PLN” VIEW OF L-INBD DISPLAY BY CLICKING ON THE CDU R-LSK “STEP” TO CYCLE THROUGH EACH WAYPOINT, AND THE WAYPOINT ON THE CDU  THAT HAS <CTR> TO THE RIGHT OF IT’S  NAME IS THE WAYPOINT THAT IS CENTERED ON THE PLAN VIEW. THE ENTIRE ROUTE SHOULD BE A CONTINUOS AND SMOOTH LINE FROM START TO FINISH WITHOUT GLITCHES IN IT.

 

 

 

IF ROUTE HAS NOT ALREADY BEEN SAVED TO CAPTAIN SIM DATABASE, ON CDU, CLICK RTE, THEN CLICK SAVE ROUTE, THEN TYPE A SHORT NAME (IF THE NAME IS THE SAME AS A PREVIOUSLY SAVED ROUTE, IT WILL BE OVERWRITTEN), THEN CLICK L-LSK NEXT TO EMPTY SQUARES TO PASTE NAME, THEN CLICK “EXEC”

PUSHBACK CHECKLIST

 

 

 

RIGHT CLICK, SELECT OUTSIDE/SPOT

 

 

 

ON SIMICON PANEL, CLICK “PLANE” ICON FOR AIRPLANE ANIMATIONS

 

 

 

PRESS “CONTROL” AND “J” ON KEYBOARD TO RETRACT JETWAY FROM MAIN DOOR, IT SHOULD ACTIVATE AND MOVE SLOWLY BACK

 

 

 

VERIFY NO TRUCKS ARE PARKED AT ANY OPEN DOORS, OTHERWISE WIAT UNTIL IT LEAVES

 

 

 

DESELECT  ALL “CARGO DOORS” AND ALL “SERVICE DOORS”. ALL DESELECTED ICONS SHOULD NOT BE GREEN

 

 

 

DESELECT  “MAIN DOOR” AND “STEWARDESS”. ALL DESELECTED ICONS SHOULD NOT BE GREEN

 

 

 

RIGHT CLICK, SELECT COCKPIT/VIRTUAL COCKPIT

 

 

 

VERIFY “DOOR” SYNOPTIC ON COCKPIT DISPLAY FOR “ALL DOORS CLOSED” IF DESIRED

 

 

 

ON DISPLAY PANEL, SELECT “CHKL”. CONTINUE WITH ELECTRONIC CHECKLIST UNTIL “BEFORE TAKEOFF”

 

 

 

DISPLAY “ATC”, SELECT “REQUEST TAXI” AND ACKNOWLEGE

 

 

 

VERIFY ATC ASSIGNED RUNWAY IS THE DEPARTING RUNWAY CONFIGURED IN FMC/CDU, RECONFIGURE CDU IF NEEDED AND VERIFY LEGS INTEGRITY AND CORRECT IF NEEDED

 

 

 

MOVE COCKPIT VIEW TO DISPLAY MCP / AP, OR ON SIMICON PANEL, CLICK “MCP”. LOOK UP MAGNETIC HEADING FOR THE ATC ASSIGNED  RUNWAY AND SET MCP / AP HEADING TO THAT VALUE

SELECT “TURN ON PROGRESSIVE TAXI” FOR DIRECTION ARROWS

 

 

 

TURN OFF “ATC”-

 

 

 

RIGHT CLICK, SELECT OUTSIDE/TOPDOWN VIEW

 

 

 

VERIFY THAT TRUCK PATHS ARE CLEAR AND DETERMINE BEST PUSHBACK DIRECTION

 

 

 

ON KEYBOARD, PRESS CONTROL “.” TO RELEASE PARKING BRAKE

 

 

 

PUSHBACK BY PRESSING SHIFT “P”, AND “1” FOR TAIL LEFT TURN, “2” FOR TAIL RIGHT TURN, OR NO NUMBER FOR STRAIGHT BACK. WAIT FOR THE PUSHBACK TO START

 

 

 

PRESS SHIFT “P” TO STOP PUSHBACK WHEN IN BEST POSITION

TAKEOFF PROCEDURE

 

 

 

ON KEYBOARD, PRESS CONTROL “.” TO RELEASE PARKING BRAKE

 

 

 

AFTER MOVING INTO POSITION ON RUNWAY, LEAVE THROTTLE LEVER AT IDLE

 

 

 

ON MCP, SET A/T ON, THIS WILL START ENGINE SPOOL UP AND ACCELERATION

 

 

 

ON PFD/AIR SPEED, VERIFY V1 DECISION SPEED REACHED

 

 

 

ON PFD/AIR SPEED, AT VR,  ROTATE TO 8 DEGREES

 

 

 

WHEN CLEAR OF RUNWAY INCREASE TO 18 DEGREES

 

 

 

ON PFD/VERTSPD INDICATOR, AT POSITIVE RATE OF CLIMB, SET LANDING GEAR UP

 

 

 

ON PFD/AIR SPEED, AT V2+10, IMMEDIATELY ADJUST AIR SPEED SMOOTHLY TO 250 KNOTS ON MCP, THIS DISPLAY WILL DISAPPEAR WHEN VNAV ACTIVATES, QUICKLY MONITOR AIR SPEED FOR FLAPS RETRACTION MARKS

 

 

 

ON PFD/AIR ALTITUDE, WHEN AT 200 FEET ABOVE RUNWAY, ON MCP SELECT A/P ON

 

 

 

ON PRD/AIR SPEED, JUST BEFORE FLAPS 5 DEGREES, RETRACT FLAPS TO 5 DEGREES

 

 

 

ON PFD/AIR SPEED, JUST BEFORE FLAPS 1 DEGREE, RETRACT FLAPS TO 1 DEGREE

 

 

 

THEN RETRACT FLAPS TO FULL UP

 

 

 

AFTER TAKEOFF CHECKLIST

 

 

 

PERFORM “AFTER TAKE OFF” ELECTRONIC CHECKLIST

 

 

 

VERIFY A/P PARAMETERS ACTIVATE

 

 

 

VERIFY FLAPS CHECK RETRACTED

 

 

 

VERIFY AUTOBRAKE OFF

 

 

 

VERIFY ASSIGNED ALTITUDE SET

 

 

 

VERIFY THAT LNAV IS ON IT SHOULD BE BANKING IN THE SAME DIRECTION AS NEW HEADING ASSIGNED FROM ATC, AND WILL CONTINUE TOWARDS ROUTE

 

 

 

WHEN ATC COMMANDS A NEW ALTITUDE, USE THUMBWHEEL OR LEFT CLICK TO CHANGE ALTITUDE, THEN PRESS “FLCH”, THIS WILL ACTIVATE FMC TO CHANGE ALTITUDE. AFTERWARDS, REACTIVATE “VNAV” WHEN NEARING THE ASSIGNED ALTITUDE

 

 

 

VERIFY BELOW 10‘000FT MAX. SPEED 250 KNOTS

 

 

 

ABOVE 10,000 FT, ON PFD, VERIFY AIR SPEED ACCELERATING TO 315 KNOTS OR IF AIR SPEED IS DISPLAYED ON MCP AND IS NOT SET CORRECTLY (DURING FLCH), SET AIR SPEED TO 315 KNOTS WITH THUMBWHEEL (WITH VNAV ACTIVATED, AIR SPEED DISPLAY WILL BE AUTOMATIC AND BLANK ON MCP)

 

 

 

ABOVE 10,000 FT, VERIFY OR SET LANDING LIGHTS OFF

 

 

 

WHEN VIEWING THE FLIGHT FROM OUTSIDE/SPOT VIEW, USE SIMICON BLUE ICONS TO AT LEAST SET UP PFD IN UPPER LEFT AND ND IN LOWER RIGHT OF YOUR MONITOR TO MONITOR FLIGHT PARAMETERS, AND CENTER BLUE ICONS TO MONITOR EICAS AND SECONDARY ENGINE DISPLAY. SELECT SIMICON “MCP” WHILE IN THIS VIEW TO MAKE CHANGES PER ATC WIGHOUT HAVING TO GO BACK INTO THE COCKPIT

VERIFY FMC HAS NO MESSAGES

 

 

 

VERIFY ENGINE, INSTRUMENTS, AND FUEL QUANTITY CHECK ON EICAS NOTE: CENTER TANK EMPTIES FIRST, SO TURN OFF CENTER PUMPS WHEN WARNING SHOWS UP ON EICAS

 

 

 

ON MCP, AUTOPILOT CHECK AND SET

APPROACH DESCENT CHECKLIST

 

 

 

SWITCH TO VIRTUAL COCKPIT

 

 

 

FOLLOW ATC COMMANDS, WHEN ATC COMMANDS A HEADING CHANGE, CHANGE THE HEADING IN THE MCP DISPLAY FIRST, THEN SELECT “SEL” ON THE HEADING KNOB TO SWITCH FROM “LNAV” TO HEADING. FROM THIS POINT ON, LNAV WILL REMAIN OFF

 

 

 

WHEN ATC ASSIGNS DESTINATION RUNWAY FOR LANDING, GO TO “DEP ARR” ON CDU, AND ACTIVATE THE CORRECT RUNWAY IF DIFFERENT. THEN IT IS EXTREMELY IMPORTANT TO SELECT “LEGS” ON CDU AND CORRECT ANY ERRORS.

 

 

 

ALSO VERIFY THAT THE DESTINATION RUNWAY IN THE LEGS HAS THE CORRECT RUNWAY ALTITUDE BY COMPARING IT WITH THE ELEVATION FOR THAT AIRPORT IN THE EFB

 

 

 

IT IS ALSO EXTREMELY IMPORTANT TO SELECT “NAV RAD” AND VERIFY THAT THE ILS FREQUENCY THAT IS DISPLAYED IS THE CORRECT FREQUENCY BY COMPARING IT TO THE AIRPORT INFORMATION FOR THE ASSIGNED RUNWAY IN THE EFB

 

 

 

ALSO VERIFY THAT THE DESTINATION RUNWAY IN THE LEGS HAS THE CORRECT VREF SPEED FOR LANDING BY COMPARING IT WITH THE APPROACH PAGE ON THE CDU

 

 

 

WHEN DESCENDING, FOLLOW ATC COMMANDS FOR ALTITUDE AND ALTIMETER CHANGES, USE “FLCH” AND KEEP THE VNAV ENABLED

 

 

 

VERIFY THAT THE AUTOTHROTTLE IS STILL ON, OR LEAVE IT OFF AND CONTROL THE THROTTLE MANUALLY

 

 

 

BELOW 10,000 FEET, SET “LANDING” LIGHTS AND “TURN OFF” LIGHTS ON, AND VERIFY THAT AIRSPEED  DISPLAYED ON PFD IS 250 KNOTS, OR ADJUST ON MCP IF NECESSARY
